All Certificates & Licenses Required

Complete list for cloud kitchen in Bangalore, Karnataka
Order of Priority: Start with FSSAI Registration first (takes 7-15 days). Apply for GST simultaneously. Other certificates can be done in parallel.
1

FSSAI Registration (Food License)

MANDATORY - Cannot operate without this
Which type do you need? For annual turnover up to ₹12 Lakh → FSSAI Basic Registration (this is what you need to start). Once turnover crosses ₹12 Lakh → upgrade to FSSAI State License.
1

Visit FSSAI Portal

Go to foscos.fssai.gov.in → Click "Apply for License/Registration"

2

Create Account & Select "Registration"

Sign up with mobile + email → Select "Registration" (not License) → Choose "Catering / Cloud Kitchen"

3

Fill Application Form A

Business name, address (Roopena Agrahara), proprietor details, food category (non-veg meals)

4

Upload Documents

  • Aadhaar card of proprietor
  • PAN card
  • Passport size photo
  • Proof of business address (rent agreement / utility bill)
  • List of food items to be sold
5

Pay Fee & Submit

Fee: ₹100/year for basic registration (1-5 year options). Usually ₹500 for 5 years.

6

Get Registration Certificate

Processing time: 7-15 working days. You'll get a 14-digit FSSAI number. Display this in your kitchen & on all packaging.

Important: You MUST print the FSSAI number on all packaging and display the certificate in the kitchen. Zomato & Swiggy will not onboard you without a valid FSSAI number.
2

GST Registration

Required for Zomato/Swiggy even if turnover is low
Why needed immediately? Even though GST threshold is ₹20L turnover in Karnataka, Zomato & Swiggy require GST number for restaurant onboarding. GST rate for restaurants (non-AC, no liquor): 5% without ITC.
1

Visit GST Portal

Go to gst.gov.in → Services → Registration → New Registration

2

Fill Part A

PAN, mobile number, email → You'll receive OTP on both → Submit to get TRN (Temporary Reference Number)

3

Fill Part B with Documents

  • PAN card
  • Aadhaar card
  • Business address proof (rent agreement + utility bill)
  • Bank account details (cancelled cheque / passbook)
  • Photo of proprietor
  • HSN Code: 9963 (Food & beverage services)
4

Verification & GSTIN

E-sign with Aadhaar OTP → Processing: 3-7 working days → You'll receive GSTIN

GST Filing: You'll need to file GST returns monthly (GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B). Consider hiring a CA or use ClearTax/Zoho for ₹500-1000/month. As a 5% restaurant, you CANNOT claim Input Tax Credit.
3

Update Existing Udyam Certificate

Add cloud kitchen activity to family member's existing Udyam
Since Udyam is under a family member's name: You have 2 options. Either add cloud kitchen as additional activity to their existing Udyam OR register a new Udyam under your own name. If your family member's Udyam is a sole proprietorship, you CANNOT add a different person's business to it. Better to get your own.

Option A: Add Activity to Existing Udyam

1

Login to Udyam Portal

Go to udyamregistration.gov.in → Click "Update Udyam Registration"

2

Enter Udyam Number + OTP

Enter existing Udyam number → OTP will be sent to registered mobile

3

Add New Activity (NIC Code)

Add NIC Code: 56101 (Restaurants and mobile food service activities) → Add new unit address (Roopena Agrahara kitchen)

4

Submit & Download Updated Certificate

Changes reflect immediately. Download updated certificate.

Option B: Register New Udyam (Recommended)

1

Register Fresh Udyam Under Your Aadhaar

Go to udyamregistration.gov.in → "For New Entrepreneurs" → Enter your Aadhaar + PAN

2

Fill Details

Business name (your chosen cloud kitchen name) → NIC Code: 56101 → Investment & turnover details → Cloud kitchen address

3

Get Udyam Certificate

Instant - Free of cost. You'll get Udyam Registration Number immediately.

Benefits of Udyam: MSME benefits, easier bank loans, government scheme eligibility, tax benefits. It's FREE and instant - no reason not to get your own.
4

Rent Agreement (Pending!)

DO THIS FIRST - Most other registrations need this
You mentioned agreement is not done yet. This is blocking! Almost every registration (FSSAI, GST, Zomato, Swiggy) needs proof of business address. Get this done ASAP.
1

Draft Rent Agreement

Must mention: commercial use / food business / cloud kitchen operation explicitly

2

Get it on Stamp Paper

Karnataka: ₹200-500 stamp paper. Both parties sign. Needs 2 witnesses.

3

Register Online (Optional but Recommended)

Register on kaveri.karnataka.gov.in (Karnataka e-stamp portal). Registration fee: 1% of annual rent. Registered agreements are legally stronger.

4

Keep Multiple Copies

You'll need copies for: FSSAI, GST, Zomato, Swiggy, Bank account, and Trade License

5

BBMP Trade License

Municipal license to operate commercial food business in Bangalore
1

Apply Online on BBMP Portal

Go to bbmponline.karnataka.gov.in → Trade License → New Application

2

Documents Needed

  • Address proof of premises

  • NOC from landlord
  • 2 passport photos
  • Property tax receipt (ask landlord)
3

Pay Fee & Get License

Fee: ₹500-2000 (depends on area). Processing: 7-15 days. Annual renewal needed.

Zomato Restaurant Partner Onboarding

Complete step-by-step process
Zomato Commission: 18-25% per order (varies by plan). They deduct commission + GST on commission before paying you. Payout cycle: Weekly (every Monday for previous week's orders).
1

Register on Zomato for Business

Visit zomato.com/business or download "Zomato Restaurant Partner" app from Play Store. Click "Register Your Restaurant".

2

Fill Basic Details

  • Restaurant name (your cloud kitchen name)
  • Complete address with PIN code (Roopena Agrahara, 560068)
  • Restaurant type: "Cloud Kitchen / Delivery Only"
  • Cuisine: North Indian, Biryani, Mughlai
  • Owner name, mobile number, email
3

Upload Documents

  • FSSAI License/Registration (14-digit number)
  • GST Certificate (GSTIN number)
  • PAN Card
  • Bank Account Details (for payouts - cancelled cheque)
  • Menu (items with prices)
  • Restaurant Photos (kitchen setup)
4

Zomato Verification Visit

Zomato team may visit your kitchen to verify hygiene standards. Keep kitchen clean, organized, and have all documents ready. They check:

  • Kitchen cleanliness & hygiene
  • Proper storage of raw materials
  • Handwash facility
  • Separate veg/non-veg preparation areas (ideal)
  • FSSAI certificate displayed
5

Menu Setup on Zomato Dashboard

Once approved, log into Zomato Restaurant Dashboard:

  • Add menu items with names, descriptions, prices
  • Upload food photos (Zomato may offer FREE photoshoot - ask!)
  • Set item availability (lunch/dinner/all day)
  • Mark items as Veg/Non-Veg/Egg
  • Set preparation time for each item
  • Add add-ons & combos
6

Set Operating Hours

Set your operating hours: 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM. You can go offline anytime from the dashboard if you run out of stock.

7

Delivery Radius Setup

Zomato will set a default delivery radius (~5-7 km from Roopena Agrahara). This covers: Electronic City, BTM Layout, HSR Layout, Bommanahalli, Madiwala area.

8

Go Live!

Total onboarding time: 7-15 days after document submission. Once live, manage orders from the Zomato Restaurant Partner app.

Zomato Tips for New Restaurants:
  • First 2 weeks: Zomato gives new restaurants better visibility (boost period)
  • Accept ALL orders in the first month - high acceptance rate = better ranking
  • Keep preparation time LOW (under 20 min) - faster delivery = more orders
  • Reply to all reviews (especially negative ones) professionally
  • Enable Zomato Gold/Pro deals initially to attract first-time customers
  • Offer combos/meal deals - they rank higher in search
Zomato Payment Cycle: Zomato pays weekly every Monday. First payout may take 2-3 weeks after going live. They deduct: Commission (18-25%) + GST on commission (18%) + TDS (1%). Keep cash reserve for raw materials during this gap.

Food Waste Prevention Strategy

Zero waste approach for first month
1

Cook in Small Batches

Don't cook everything at once. Make gravy base, then cook portioned amounts as orders come in.

2

Go Offline When Low Stock

If only 3 portions of mutton left, mark "Mutton Curry" as unavailable on Zomato/Swiggy. Better than disappointing customers with cancellations.

3

Repurpose Leftover Gravy

Leftover chicken gravy → becomes base for next day's biryani masala. Leftover rice → egg fried rice next day.

4

Track Everything

Maintain a simple notebook: Date | Item | Prepared | Sold | Wasted. This data will tell you exactly how much to cook each day.

Phase 2: Adding Alcohol to Food

PHASE 2 - After 2-3 Months
Important Distinction: Using alcohol as a cooking ingredient (wine in gravy, beer-battered chicken, rum in desserts) is DIFFERENT from serving alcohol as a beverage. The rules and licenses are completely different.
🍳

Option A: Alcohol as Cooking Ingredient

Wine-based gravies, beer-battered items, rum desserts
This is the easier path for cloud kitchens. Many restaurants use wine, beer, and spirits in cooking without needing a liquor license.

Menu Ideas with Alcohol-Infused Cooking:

ItemAlcohol UsedPrice Range
Red Wine Mutton CurryRed wine in gravy₹380-450
Beer Butter ChickenBeer in butter sauce₹280-350
Whiskey Glazed ChickenWhiskey reduction glaze₹320-400
Beer Battered Chicken FryBeer in batter₹250-320
Rum & Coke RibsRum + cola marinade₹350-450
Wine-Braised Mutton BiryaniRed wine in biryani stock₹400-500

What You Need:

1

FSSAI Compliance

FSSAI allows alcohol as a food ingredient. Your food label/packaging MUST clearly state: "Contains alcohol-based ingredients". This is mandatory.

2

Update FSSAI Registration

Update your food items list on FSSAI to include alcohol-infused dishes. Log in to foscos.fssai.gov.in → Modify/Update → Add new food items.

3

Source Alcohol from Licensed Shop

Buy from any licensed wine/liquor shop. Keep purchase bills as proof for excise department if they ever ask. No special wholesale license needed for small quantities used in cooking.

4

Zomato/Swiggy Menu Update

Add new items with clear description: "Cooked with [wine/beer/rum]". Add a note about alcohol content. Some platforms may flag this - coordinate with your account manager.

5

Packaging Labels

Update packaging to include: Ingredient list with alcohol mentioned, "Not suitable for minors" advisory, and FSSAI number.

Karnataka Excise Note: Karnataka has strict excise rules. While cooking with alcohol is generally permitted, keep records of all alcohol purchases. Store alcohol securely in kitchen. If Karnataka excise department inspects, show them purchase receipts and explain usage is for cooking only. Amount used per dish is small (50-100ml per batch).
🚫

Option B: Serving Alcohol as Beverage (NOT Recommended for Cloud Kitchen)

For reference only - very complex and expensive
Why NOT recommended: Cloud kitchens typically CANNOT get liquor serving licenses. Karnataka requires physical dining space, and Zomato/Swiggy do NOT deliver alcohol in most cities. This option is only viable if you later convert to a dine-in restaurant.

For future reference, if you ever expand to dine-in:

Recommended Path: Start with Phase 1 (regular cloud kitchen) → After 2-3 months, add alcohol-infused cooking (Option A) → If business grows well and you want to expand, consider dine-in + liquor license in Year 2.
